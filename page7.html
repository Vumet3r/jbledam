Room acoustic, acoustic transducers and vibroacoustic

Because we maybe don’t know the reason but we don’t want to go to this bar, and it is simple, its for the noise.

But if you keep looking for acoustical error I will told you the bigguest one. No too much time ago some architect decided to build a cilinder assembly hall in the university of Alicante.

Everybody know the whisper gallery of London right? Who better than Lord Rayleigh[1910] for explain what that meaning?

That’s it, They hire and architect whose name I don’t want to remember who build a marvellous whisper hall. This was one of the proyect that we develop during the subjetc Acoustic room desinging. In the simulation at CATT Acoustic,the parameters as the RASTI improved, and the Reverberation time decreases. Despite this improvement, this place it is not a concert hall anymore, but it will work for voice.

Vibroacoustic and acoustic transducers

We studied how to characterize vibratory motion, and radiant system. It is commonly use to charaterize materials, finding their density, young module or fundamental modes. We conect this knowledge with acoustic transducer, measuring active and pasive Bass-reflex and closed boxes.

Moreover we simulate some examples using FEM method, a powerfull tool to solve complex problems. We used boundary conditions to simulate properties of materials.

Every day we have got more complex acoustic transducer, it require new methods to improve the building of new age speaker, for example DML speakers which uses a new technology based in creating distributed modes.
