Recycled speaker

Premise :

You have to desing an Speaker with less than 10€, we will evaluate the THD, the SPL, and the artistic desing. We will consider the cost of the speaker, taking into the account the amount of money that we saved.

First of all we had to decide which material we were going to use, in order to avoid the fundamental modes we choose something with circular shape for the membrane. The coil had to be with the thiner copper wire to reduce the weight of the speaker with the purpose to obtain the maximum SPL.

As we didn't have machines to do the coil, and we didn’t find the cooper as thiner as we wanted we had some problems, for example, to obtain a resistance of 4 Ohm to avoid burning the CD player. I warned them about it, but my parners want to check it and this happened.

We learned a lot doing this project, later we studied a subject call Acoustic Transducers, and we were able to recognice many errors that we made.
